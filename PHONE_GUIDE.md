## 🛠️ Step 1: Install the Tool in Lemur Browser

1.  Open **Lemur Browser** on your phone.
2.  Tap the **Extension icon** (looks like a puzzle piece 🧩) at the bottom.
3.  Tap **Manage Extensions** (or "User-defined Extensions").
4.  Turn on **"Developer mode"** (switch is at the top right).
5.  Tap **Load unpacked**.
6.  Find the folder where you extracted the tool files and select it.
    *   *Success!* You should now see "Netflix Injector" appear in the list.

## 🚀 Step 2: Unlock Netflix Premium

1.  Still in **Lemur Browser**, go to [netflix.com](https://netflix.com).
2.  Tap the **Menu** (3 lines/dots) → Find **Netflix Injector**.
3.  A box will pop up. Enter your **License Key** and tap the red **Inject Cookie** button.
4.  Wait a few seconds until you see green text: **"✅ Injection Successful!"**

## ❓ Common Problems & Fixes

**"It redirects me to the Play Store!"**
> This means your phone blocked the deep link.
> **Fix 1:** Go to **Android Settings** → **Apps** → **Netflix** → **Set as Default** → Enable **"Open supported links"**.
> **Fix 2 (Force Launch):**
> 1. Copy this long text:
>    `intent://www.netflix.com/browse#Intent;scheme=https;package=com.netflix.mediaclient;end`
> 2. Paste it into your browser's address bar and hit Enter.
> 3. This tells Android SPECIFICALLY to open the Netflix App.

## 🌐 Method 2: Manual Injection (Any Browser)

If you have a way to manually inject cookies (e.g. valid "Cookie Editor" extension or app) on your phone:

1.  **On Desktop:** Click **Inject Cookie** in the extension.
2.  Once successful, click the new **"Copy JSON"** button.
3.  Send the copied text to your phone (email, notes, etc.).
4.  **On Phone:** Open your browser (Edge/Chrome/etc.).
5.  Go to `netflix.com`.
6.  Open your Cookie Editor tool.
7.  **Import** the JSON you copied.
8.  Go to this link: `https://www.netflix.com/unsupported`
9.  Tap **"Open App"**.
